Dona Paula

The upper management monkeys start paddling really hard. A bulb lights up. I say to myself -- Akshat,old firend, when a lady gives you an opportunity to see her pearl necklace you don't sit around and muse on life. These things are best reserved for a rainy day, which are plentiful by the way. This is the time to stand up and take action. The monkeys might be on to something here. Who knows when one might get lucky?

As for Dona. I think she is playing hard to get. Everybody else seems to have seen that pearl necklace but she wouldn't let me have a glimpse. Oh well plenty of fish!
