What I do?

Hello I am Akshat :) I am an Independent consultant working in gurgaon,India. I help small-medium size business with their business problems. For example:-

I helped a medium sized law firm, 17 members at that time, by providing them with a better solution (as compared to excel) to keep detailed records of the cases they undertook. These records included:-

  • If they were defending/prosecuting for a criminal charge: the charges levied against the defendant,the documents related to their defence (arguments,evidence and records of witnesses)
  • If they were defending/pleading in a civil suit: sections of the act against which the releif was sought by the plaintiff,their response (including the arguments,evidence and records of witnesses)
  • A log of dates starting from the commencement of the case right untill the end of it (including the dates where hearing, presentation of evidence etc were postponed)
  • For each day on the dashboard a list of cases to attend by a lawyer was shown
  • At the end of each month a status report was mailed to the manager which included a summary timeline of the cases the costs incurred and the fees collected.
Technologies that I used: couchdb,nginx and jquery
