Analytics for users

We can use google anaytics to track the webstes across multiple domains and subdomains. We can also pull results using the analytics api for every domain/subdomain.

There is a node js client library for GA. Bindings are also available in other languages

We should also provide simplified analytics summary for the users. The summary should be visible on a separate screen in the mobile app. Some ideas on parameters that can be tracked:-

  • From which countries he gets visits. He should be able to see the country name and the number of visitors he got from that country. Ex: India: 203
  • Number of hits per month
  • Number of chat requests
  • Number of bookings

Depending upon the success of the analytics feature we may also add other parameters to tracking:-

  • On what listings he got most hits
  • From what country/region he got his most bookings
  • How is property doing with respect to other users in the same area?
  • On what days he got overbooked?
  • On what days the bookings got cancelled?
  • What is the occupancy rate of his property?
  • What % of people who booked actually showed up?

Based on the numbers we may also provide him tips on maximizing his occupancy rate. Or at least encourage him to talk of the discussion forums so that he may get help from the community.
