Notes on collecting payments

  1. We don't have to limit ourselves to paypal. The customers will either be local/foreign
  2. For local customers we'll provide the flexibility of making payments via NEFT bank transfers, UPI, or even cheque. These payments can be made offline.
  3. For foreign customers the payments must be made online. But we can give them options to make a payment either via paypal or by a wire transfer directly into the bank

For manual payments our operational costs increase. If we'll have to talk to the customer to release the payment every time then we'll have to set a hard limit on the number of customers that we can address. That is not a problem because we'll do a research on finding the optimal number of customers anyway. We don't want to take on more than we can chew here. All the operational efforts must be planned in a way that it is possible for a single person to handle everything.
